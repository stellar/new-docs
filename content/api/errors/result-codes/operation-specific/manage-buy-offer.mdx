These are result codes that communicate success (200) or failure (400) specific to the `Manage Buy Offer` operation.

Learn more about the [`Manage Buy Offer` operation](../../../../docs/glossary/operations/manage-buy-offer.mdx).

<AttributeTable>

- OpSuccess
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_SUCCESS
  - The offer was successfully placed
- OpMalformed
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_MALFORMED
  - generated offer would be invalid
- op_sell_no_trust
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_SELL_NO_TRUST
  - no trust line for what we're selling
- op_buy_no_trust
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_BUY_NO_TRUST
  - no trust line for what we're buying
- sell_not_authorized
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_SELL_NOT_AUTHORIZED
  - not authorized to sell
- buy_not_authorized
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_BUY_NOT_AUTHORIZED
  - not authorized to buy
- OpLineFull
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_LINE_FULL
  - can't receive more of what it's buying
- OpUnderfunded
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_UNDERFUNDED
  - doesn't hold what it's trying to sell
- op_cross_self
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_CROSS_SELF
  - would cross an offer from the same user
- op_sell_no_issuer
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_SELL_NO_ISSUER
  - no issuer for what we're selling
- buy_no_issuer
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_BUY_NO_ISSUER
  - no issuer for what we're buying
- op_offer_not_found
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_NOT_FOUND
  - offerID does not match an existing offer
- OpLowReserve
  - MANAGE_BUY_OFFER_LOW_RESERVE
  - not enough funds to create a new Offer

</AttributeTable>
